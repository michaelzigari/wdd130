White Water Rafting Adventures

Our Mission:

We are dedicated to providing safe, exciting and memorable rafting experiences. Our professional guides bring years of experience and in-depth knowledge of river navigation to ensure that every trip is both thrilling and secure. We provide top-quality rafts, helmets, life jackets, and all gear needed to keep you protected on the water. Whether you are looking for an adrenaline-pumping ride or a scenic family-friend float, our mission is to connect people with the adventure of whitewater rafting while fostering respect for nature and teamwork among participants.

History

White Water Rafting Adventures began as a small passion project in the early 2000s when the founder Michael Zigari, first discovered the thrill of whitewater rafting. What started as weekend trips with friends quickly grew into guided adventures for locals and tourists alike. Over time, our reputation for safety, professionalism and unforgettable experiences turned our small team into one of the region's most trusted rafting outfitters. Today, Major Michael Rafting Adventures has expanded to multiple rivers, offering adventures for beginners, families, and expert rafters from all over the world.
